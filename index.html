What we're working on

Gorilla Showdown

Gorilla Showdown is our upcoming stylized third-person shooter rhythm game inspired by the late 90's early 2000's house and funk scenes. Fight it out to be the reining gorilla in the dance floor battle arenas.

Flickor

Flickor is an engaging single-player time-management and shopkeeping simulation game. Step into the shoes of a dedicated alchemist, tasked with brewing potions for a diverse clientele ranging from adventurous travellers to ordinary townsfolk.

About Us

We're SlothKing, an independent, new game development studio based in Melbourne, Australia.

We're currently working on Gorilla Showdown, our first game, and we're all about combining the playing with friends and creating truly unique titles to create unforgettable memories.

Come along for the ride as we turn our ideas into exciting new experiences!
